Frequently Asked Questions

What makes InvoiceKits different from other invoice generators?

InvoiceKits offers unique features like CSV batch upload for creating 100+ invoices at once, 5 professional PDF templates, recurring invoices, and API access - all with a generous free tier. Most competitors either lack batch processing or charge significantly more.

Is InvoiceKits cheaper than FreshBooks or Zoho Invoice?

Yes. InvoiceKits starts with a free plan (5 invoices/month) and paid plans from $9/month. FreshBooks starts at $17/month and Zoho Invoice at $15/month. InvoiceKits Professional ($29/month) includes features that cost $50+ on other platforms.

Can I switch from another invoice tool to InvoiceKits?

Yes! You can start fresh with InvoiceKits immediately. Export your client list from your current tool and use our batch upload feature to quickly create invoices for all your clients.

Does InvoiceKits have accounting features like Wave or QuickBooks?

InvoiceKits focuses on doing invoicing exceptionally well rather than being a full accounting suite. If you need expense tracking, bank reconciliation, or tax filing, pair InvoiceKits with your preferred accounting software via our API.

What's the difference between credits and subscriptions?

Credits are pay-as-you-go: buy them when you need them, they never expire. Subscriptions offer better value if you invoice regularly - as low as $0.15/invoice vs $0.70-0.90 with credits. Subscriptions also include premium features like batch upload and recurring invoices.

Do other invoice tools offer pay-as-you-go credits?

No. InvoiceKits is unique in offering a credit-based pay-as-you-go model. Other tools either require monthly subscriptions (Zoho, FreshBooks) or have limited free tiers with no upgrade path besides subscriptions. Our credits give you flexibility without commitment.
