Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my invoice limit?

You'll be prompted to upgrade your plan. Your existing invoices remain accessible, but you won't be able to create new ones until you upgrade or wait for the next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure payment processor, Stripe.
