Frequently Asked Questions

What is time tracking invoice software?

Time tracking invoice software combines a timer for tracking billable hours with invoicing capabilities. Instead of using separate tools for time tracking and billing, you can track time and create invoices in one place. InvoiceKits lets you start a timer, log hours, and convert time entries directly into professional invoices.

How does the bill-to-invoice feature work?

After tracking time, select the unbilled entries you want to invoice and click 'Bill Time'. You can choose to create detailed line items (one per time entry) or summarized items (grouped by description). The invoice is automatically populated with your time entries, rates, and totals.

Can I track time for multiple clients?

Yes! Each time entry can be associated with a specific client. When billing, you can filter by client and create separate invoices for each. The time entry list shows all your tracked time organized by client and status.

How many timers can I run at once?

The number of simultaneous timers depends on your plan: Free and Starter plans allow 1 active timer, Professional allows up to 5 timers, and Business plan offers unlimited simultaneous timers for teams working on multiple projects.

Can I add time entries manually?

Yes! In addition to the real-time timer, you can manually add time entries by entering hours and minutes. This is useful for logging time retroactively or when you forget to start the timer.

Does the timer work across page navigation?

Yes, the timer persists as you navigate through the application. You can start a timer on the dashboard, work on other pages, and the timer will continue running until you stop it.
