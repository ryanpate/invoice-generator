Frequently Asked Questions

What is a voice invoice generator?

A voice invoice generator lets you create invoices by speaking instead of typing. You tap a microphone button, describe your work and client details out loud, and AI automatically transcribes your speech and fills in the invoice fields — including client name, email, line items, rates, and payment terms.

What invoice details can I dictate by voice?

InvoiceKits can extract: client name, client email, client address, invoice currency, payment terms (e.g. Net 30), and all line items with descriptions, quantities, and rates. Just speak naturally and the AI figures out the structure.

Is the voice invoice generator free?

Yes! You can try it on the /try/ page without signing up. Voice generation shares the same AI quota as text generation: Free users get 3 generations/month, Starter gets 10, and Professional and Business plans include unlimited generations.

What browsers support voice invoicing?

Voice invoicing requires browser support for the MediaRecorder API. It works in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari on desktop. On mobile, it works in Chrome for Android and Safari for iOS. The mic button is automatically hidden on unsupported browsers.

How long can my voice recording be?

Recordings are limited to 60 seconds. A warning appears at 55 seconds and recording stops automatically at 60 seconds. This is more than enough to describe even a complex multi-line invoice.
