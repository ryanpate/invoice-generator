Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI invoice generator?

An AI invoice generator uses artificial intelligence to automatically create invoice line items from natural language descriptions. Instead of manually entering each item, you simply describe your work in plain English (e.g., "Website redesign, 20 hours at $100/hour") and the AI generates properly formatted invoice entries.

How accurate is the AI invoice generator?

InvoiceKits AI invoice generator is powered by Claude, one of the most advanced AI models available. It accurately parses hourly rates, fixed prices, quantities, and descriptions. You always have the opportunity to review and edit generated items before adding them to your invoice.

Is the AI invoice generator free to use?

Yes! Free users get 3 AI generations per month. Starter plan users get 10 generations per month. Professional and Business plan subscribers get unlimited AI invoice generations.

What types of work can the AI invoice generator handle?

The AI can handle virtually any type of billable work including hourly services, fixed-price projects, product sales, consulting fees, retainers, and mixed billing. It understands complex descriptions like "Logo design $500 plus 3 rounds of revisions at $100 each".

Can I edit the AI-generated invoice items?

Absolutely! The AI generates a preview of line items that you can review before adding to your invoice. You can edit descriptions, quantities, rates, or remove items entirely. The AI is a starting point that saves time, not a replacement for your judgment.
