How It Works

  • Late fees are checked daily at 7:00 AM UTC
  • Only invoices with status "Sent" or "Overdue" receive late fees
  • Each invoice receives only one late fee (no compounding)
  • You can pause late fees for individual invoices from the invoice detail page
  • Both you and your client will be notified when a late fee is applied
